Project 11

Welcome to Project Eleven, a cutting-edge robotic quadruped inspired by natural locomotion. Built using reinforcement learning and MuJoCo simulations, Eleven learns to walk, balance, and adapt just like real-world animals.

Our mission is to explore advanced robotics, control systems, and artificial intelligence through realistic and dynamic robot design.
