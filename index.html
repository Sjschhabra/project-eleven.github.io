Project Eleven
Project Eleven is an open-source robotic quadruped inspired by the agility of an American Pitbull Terrier — white with black spots, agile and strong.

Built using MuJoCo simulation and reinforcement learning, our current focus is on making the robot body stable, understanding the simulation environment, and analyzing the data collected through its sensors. We aim to capture realistic movement and behavior in simulation before proceeding to real-world deployment.
